About Our Club

Demand respect or expect defeat

Founded in 1986, the American Football Club are members of the American Football and Cheerleading Federation. In addition, the American Football Club are members of both the National Federation of Sports and the USA Sports Association.

As American Football Club we continually aspire to make our sports program attractive to all types of players, both elite and novice, and both large and small. During recent years, we have made a concerted effort to increase the access of our programs, especially in our youth program where we believe we are uniquely equipped to offer athletic activities and organizational support that alternative sports cannot provide.
